Welcome to the official website of the London Mousers & Co.

The London Mousers & Co. are an organization of cats responsible for the various tasks involved in the upkeep of the streets and many governmental offices of the city of Westminster.

Among their prestigeous ranks are such cats as the mousers themselves, responsibe for keeping the alleyways and buildings rodent-free at all times; and the fleetstreet mousers, responsible for the printing of such esteemed mewspapers as The Mouser Post and The Saturday Evening Mews; among many other sub-groups and organizations.

Find out more about our members on the about page, read the latest mews, or contact us with any questions or concerns. We hope to see you soon!

— Larry Cameron, The London Mousers
